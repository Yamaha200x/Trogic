Privacy Policy

At Trogic, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with the applicable privacy laws.

1. Data Collection

We collect personal information that is necessary for the provision of our services. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details.
  • Technical Data: Information such as IP addresses, browser type, operating system, and other similar data automatically collected when you visit our website.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, including time spent on pages, features used, and actions taken. This data helps us improve user experience.
  • Location Data: We may collect data about your location if you enable location services on your device.

2. Data Usage

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide and enhance our services, and to communicate with you.
  • Security: To ensure the security of our platform and protect against fraud or misuse.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations, including tax, accounting, and other regulatory requirements.
  • Analytics and Marketing: To analyze website usage trends, perform marketing research, and improve our offerings. We may also send promotional emails or newsletters if you have opted in.

3. Data Protection

Your privacy is important to us. We implement various technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • Encryption: All sensitive information (such as payment details) is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols to ensure safe transmission over the internet.
  • Access Control: We restrict access to personal data to authorized personnel only, ensuring that your data is kept confidential.
  • Regular Security Audits: We perform regular audits to ensure our systems are secure and compliant with privacy laws.
  • Data Retention: We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we may share your data in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We may share your data with trusted third-party providers who help us run our business, such as hosting providers, analytics services, and email providers. These partners are obligated to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process, we may disclose your personal data to authorities.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal data may be transferred as part of the transaction.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your user experience and collect usage data:

  • Essential Cookies: These are required for the operation of the website (e.g., session cookies).
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with the website, enabling us to improve functionality and performance.
  • Advertising Cookies: These are used to display targeted advertisements based on your browsing behavior.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time, though some features of the website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

6. Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain exceptions (e.g., legal requirements).
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails.
  • Data Portability: You can request to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used format to transfer it to another service.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at info@trogic.com.

We are committed to responding to all privacy-related inquiries in a timely and professional manner.
